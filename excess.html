Mortality in Sweden 2020¶

Charts that show mortality in 2020 compared to 2015-2019. Total numbers published weekly by Statistiska Centralbyrån. Covid reported deaths from Folkhälsomyndigheten updated Tuesday-Friday.

Due to reporting lag the data for the latest two weeks in 2020 will change. This data is not shown in the chart.

Cumulative deaths 2015-2020 by County¶

An illustration of total deaths in 2020 per county compared to 2015-2019. Last two weeks of data in 2020 are shown as points since they will change.

Weekly deaths with filter on county level¶

Click on a county marker in the legend to the right to include/exclude it in the total numbers. Data from latest two weeks in 2020 are excluded.
