Weekly reported deaths¶

Number of deaths reported each week with each publication weekday shown individually. Since week 2 no numbers are published on weekends. Since week 38 numbers are only published Tuesdays trough Fridays.

Weekly reported cases¶

Same chart as above but showing cases instead of deaths. Cases are reported daily.

Why is the total number different from official numbers?¶

Total death toll in these graphs differ from official total numbers since:

  • Old data points with few deaths have been trimmed to make graphs more legible
  • Some death dates are unknown and are not included in these numbers
